Los Angeles based fashion and portrait photographer whose work is grounded in conveying the strength and vulnerability of women. Through her use of rich color palettes and intimate imagery, Maguire seeks to create a visceral experience for her viewers that is both deeply personal and socially relevant, incorporating fashion as both a visual and formal aspect of her work, exploring the way in which clothing can be used to both empower and expose.
